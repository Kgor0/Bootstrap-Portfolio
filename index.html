About

Here's more information about our workspace.

  • Why we code

    Coding teaches you how to think. During the coding process, you need to give a solution to an issue that involves intensive problem-solving skills. It trains your brain to think deeply, identfy the troubles, and break things up, then piece them together with a solution.

  • Any one can code!

    That's right! Even children can code. Understanding computers and learning the basics of coding helps chidren develop an appreciation of how things work. It also teaches them how software engineers use math in order to solve problems in a logical and creative way.

  • Teamwork makes the Dreamwork

    Coding is a lot more fun because you are able to code with a team of people. You can check each others work out and learn from the people around you. This will make you a better developer in the long run.

  • September 2022

    End of year internships

    Companies are always looking for interns at the end of the year. Provided you are up to date, have completed all your projects and you're a coding wiz, you could have an opportunity to intern at a huge company. We encourage students to constantly work hard.
